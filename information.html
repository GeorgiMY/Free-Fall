All About the Project

Project Philosophy

Why did I create it?

Since I was young, I've been extremely motivated to understand how and why the universe works the way it does. Years ago, I reached the conclusion that physics is the best way to learn this. One of the concepts in physics that I'm very interested in is gravity.

Project Goals

The main goal is to help students and teachers. To help students understand what gravity is, and to give teachers a tool that allows them to visualize it.
The second goal is to motivate and encourage people to study physics, and to help enthusiasts and those already interested by making the learning process easier.

What is what? What does each object property in the project serve?

The Arrow

The arrow above the object shows the direction of movement, and its length shows the speed. The higher the speed, the longer the arrow. The arrow only appears if the object is in motion.

The Trail

This is the trace that an object leaves when it moves. After approximately a minute, the trail begins to disappear.
